About Pet-O-Pedia

Pet-O-Pedia is an online platform for people who loves animals, especially pets. Here you can easily detect the animals that you have, to gain informations you might need.

Founded in 2024, our commitment is to give people who access this website a broad knowledge about their animals. It can be recommended food, common disease, etc.

Who Are We?

We are a group of people who are committed to provide people with insights about the animals they own, in order to provide benefits and satisfaction to both the owners and the animals they keep.

Our Goals

Give people knowledge about the animals they have.

One of our goals is to provide the informations related to animals, especially pets. So that people can get to know their animals better.
